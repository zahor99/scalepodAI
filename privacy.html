Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

ScalePod AI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the scalepod.ai website and the ScalePod AI platform (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service:

  • Personal Data: Email address, name, and contact information you provide when signing up for our waitlist, creating an account, or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: Information on how the Service is accessed and used, including your browser type, IP address, device type, operating system, pages visited, time spent, and referring URL.
  • API Credentials: If you connect third-party services (e.g., Printify, Etsy), we securely store the API keys and tokens necessary to operate the Service on your behalf.
  • Product Data: Design briefs, product descriptions, images, tags, and other content you create using the Service.
  • Transaction Data: Payment information, billing address, and purchase history related to your subscription.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Service
  • To notify you about changes, updates, and new features
  • To provide customer support
  • To gather analytics to improve the Service
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • To process transactions and send billing-related communications
  • To personalize your experience and deliver relevant content
  • To serve and measure the effectiveness of advertisements on third-party platforms (see Section 5)

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom (UK), or Switzerland, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent for a specific purpose (e.g., joining our waitlist, subscribing to emails, accepting non-essential cookies).
  • Contract: Where processing is necessary to perform our contract with you (e.g., providing the Service).
  • Legitimate interests: Where processing is necessary for our legitimate business interests, such as improving the Service, fraud prevention, and marketing, provided these interests do not override your rights.
  • Legal obligation: Where we need to process your data to comply with applicable law.

You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at wellnesspresso@gmail.com. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase (PostgreSQL) for data storage with Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure data isolation between users. API keys and sensitive credentials are encrypted at rest.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL), access controls, and regular security reviews.

We may use third-party advertising and analytics services to promote our Service across the Internet, measure ad effectiveness, and understand how visitors interact with our website. These third-party vendors, including Google, Meta (Facebook/Instagram), and Pinterest, may display our ads on sites and apps across the Internet based on your prior visits to our website.

Technologies used may include:

  • Google Ads & Google Analytics: We may use Google Ads remarketing and Google Analytics to show ads to visitors who have previously visited our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your past visits. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram): We may use the Meta Pixel to measure ad effectiveness, optimize ad delivery, and build audiences for future ads. This technology may collect data about your browsing activity. Subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Pinterest Tag: We may use the Pinterest Tag to track conversions, optimize ad campaigns, and build audiences on Pinterest. Subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

How to opt out of personalized advertising:

  • Google: Visit Google Ads Settings or install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

  • Pinterest: Visit Pinterest Privacy Settings
  • General opt-out: Visit Digital Advertising Alliance or Your Online Choices (EU)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect and track information about activity on our Service. Cookies are small data files placed on your device.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the Service to function (session management, security, preferences). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the Service (e.g., Google Analytics). These are used only with your consent where required by law.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by third-party advertising partners (Google, Meta, Pinterest) to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign performance. These are used only with your consent where required by law.

Managing cookies: You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when cookies are being sent. If you disable or refuse cookies, some parts of the Service may become inaccessible or not function properly. For users in the EEA/UK, we will ask for your consent before placing non-essential cookies.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this policy:

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active, and for up to 30 days after account deletion to allow for recovery.
  • Product data: Retained as long as your account is active. Deleted upon request or within 90 days of account termination.
  • Usage and analytics data: Retained in anonymized or aggregated form for up to 26 months.
  • Waitlist data: Retained until the Service launches and you either create an account or request deletion.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at wellnesspresso@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or sooner if required by applicable law).

  • Right to opt-out of sale/sharing: We do not sell your personal information. If we share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising (e.g., through advertising pixels), you have the right to opt out. Use the opt-out links in Section 5 above.

To submit a request, email us at wellnesspresso@gmail.com. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.
