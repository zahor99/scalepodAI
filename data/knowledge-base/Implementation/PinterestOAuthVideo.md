  #Key Tips for Approval

  1. Show the full OAuth redirect — Pinterest must see the consent screen
  with your app name and the redirect back. Don't cut or skip it.
  2. Show real content being created — use an actual product image, not
  lorem ipsum. They want to see your app has a real use case.
  3. Keep it under 2 minutes — they review thousands of these. Short and
  clear wins.
  4. No voiceover needed — the UI flow speaks for itself. If you want one,
  just narrate what's happening in each step.
  5. MP4 format, under 2GB — OBS defaults to MKV, change output to MP4 in
  Settings → Output → Recording Format.
  6. Make sure the URL bar is visible — they want to see the OAuth redirect
  URLs (your app → pinterest.com → back to your app).
