## Design Notes

### Common Patterns
- All dresses use **95% Polyester / 5% Spandex** four-way stretch sublimation fabric
- Production time is consistently **4 days (90%)**, **2.5 days average**
- Panel names are accessible via `img[alt]` attributes on piece icons in the sidebar
- Resolution info appears in the canvas area as "Max W*H px" when a panel is selected
- "Lace" panels are decorative ruffle/trim strips — very wide and narrow

### Panel Count by Complexity
| Product | Panels | Complexity |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Sleeveless Cami Dress | 3 | Simple (front + back + lace strip) |
| V-Neck Short Sleeve Dress | 8 | Complex (multi-section front/back + placket + collar) |
| Stacked Ruffle Sleeve Dress | 10 | Most complex (front/back + sleeves + collars + laces) |

### Artwork Reuse Strategy
- **Front/Back panels** are always the largest and most important — hero artwork goes here
- **Sleeve panels** (when present) can use a complementary pattern or scaled version of hero
- **Collar strips** are very narrow — simple patterns, gradient, or solid colors work best
- **Lace/trim strips** are extremely elongated — seamless horizontal repeat patterns ideal
- **Left/Right sleeve** panels have identical dimensions — mirror or duplicate artwork
- **Inner/Outer collar** pairs have nearly identical dimensions — can use same artwork
