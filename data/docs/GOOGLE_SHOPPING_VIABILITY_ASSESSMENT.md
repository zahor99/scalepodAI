# Google Shopping Viability Assessment — AOP Dresses (PeaPrint/Yoycol)

> Created: 2026-03-05 | Method: Visual Google Shopping browsing + keyword data analysis

## Executive Summary

After visually verifying 8 keyword × product combinations on Google Shopping and analyzing CPC/margin feasibility, here's the bottom line:

**VIABLE niches for AOP dresses on Google Shopping:**
1. **Dark Floral Dress** — Best opportunity. High volume, low CPC, retail competition (not AOP). We'd be the affordable AOP alternative.
2. **Whimsigoth Dress** — Niche but highly targeted. Low CPC, less competition, our ICP exactly.
3. **Celestial Dress** — Good opportunity. Mixed competition, less saturated than others.
4. **Boho Floral Maxi Dress** — Viable with bright colorful designs (NOT dark aesthetic). Many printed/sublimation competitors already.

**NOT VIABLE:**
5. **Cottagecore Dress** — Entirely cut-and-sew garments (corsets, lace, puff sleeves, embroidery). AOP cannot compete.
6. **Gothic Dress (generic)** — Dominated by Shein/ROMWE at $17-33. Price war we can't win.
7. **All Over Print Dress** — Near-zero search volume (50/mo). Nobody searches this way.
8. **Matching Sets** — Too broad, pulls in pajamas/swimwear/loungewear. High CPC ($0.60).

**Best PeaPrint products for this niche:**

### 5. Cottagecore Dress ❌ NOT VIABLE

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Volume | 6,600/mo (peak 8,100 in spring) |
| CPC | $0.28 |
| Competition type | 100% cut-and-sew (corsets, lace, puff sleeves, tiered, embroidery) |
| Price range on GS | $29-$87 |
| Our price point | Would be $34.99-$44.99 |

**What we saw:** EVERY result for "cottagecore dress" is a structurally constructed garment — corset lacing, smocked bodices, puff sleeves, tiered layers, embroidery. Materials filter shows Chiffon, Silk, Lace, Cotton. ZERO AOP/sublimation results.

**Why NOT viable:**
- Buyer intent is for garment construction features (corset, lace-up, puff sleeves) that AOP flat printing cannot replicate
- Our flat-printed polyester dress would look completely wrong next to the competition
- Even if we rank, click-through and conversion would be near zero — product mismatch
- Despite high volume and low CPC, this is a **keyword-product mismatch**

**Verdict:** Do NOT target cottagecore keywords on Google Shopping. Can still use cottagecore *motifs* (ditsy florals, strawberries, daisies) under other keywords like "floral print dress" if silhouette matches.

### Ad Feasibility Verdict

**Only two niches have clearly profitable ad economics:**

1. **Whimsigoth + $39.99-$49.99 products** — Break-even CVR of 1.9-2.2%, well within achievable range. Best ROAS potential of any niche.

2. **Dark Floral + $49.99 Elegant Long Dress** — Break-even CVR of 2.4%, achievable for a differentiated product in a retail-dominated SERP.

**Borderline (test with small budget):**
- Dark Floral + $39.99 products (2.8% CVR needed — possible if mockups are exceptional)
- Celestial + $49.99 products (3.1% — may work during peak season Nov-Dec)

**Not profitable for ads:**
- Everything at $34.99 or below
- Boho at any price point (low volume + high CPC)
- Matching sets (CPC too high)
- Cottagecore / Generic gothic (product mismatch / price war)
